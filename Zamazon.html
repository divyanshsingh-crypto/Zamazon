Who We Are

India's Wildest Trails, Guided Safely

Zamazon is a tour guide company that helps you reach different and difficult treks and mountain peaks — making your journey to the destination easier and far more comfortable. From the Himalayas to the Western Ghats, we've charted every trail worth climbing.

Trekking in India offers vast landscapes and amazing terrain. The trend has gained massive momentum, with tourists from all over the world visiting famous trails. Our routes are rated easy, moderate, and tough — so every explorer finds the right challenge.

Be Prepared

Gear & Essentials

Everything you need before you step onto the trail. We've compiled the complete list from seasoned mountaineers.

⛏️

Climbing Gear

Ice axe, hammer, pulley, pegs and slings, crampons — the core arsenal for any serious ascent.

🏕️

Shelter & Sleep

Lightweight tent and a warm sleeping bag rated for sub-zero temperatures. Never compromise here.

🧭

Navigation

Compass, torch light, and whistle. Old-school tools that save lives when technology fails.

🎒

Trekking Pack

Sturdy backpack with proper load distribution, trekking shoes for grip, and trek pants for comfort.

💧

Hydration & Fuel

Water bottle is non-negotiable. Pack high-calorie snacks to keep hunger pangs at bay on long ascents.

🩹

Safety & Health

Personal medical kit, hand sanitizer, rope, and a documented medical history shared with your guide.
